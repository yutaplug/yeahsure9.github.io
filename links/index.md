# Fix opening Discord links
## Samsung
- Go to Settings app
- Go to `Apps` section
- Search for Discord & click on it
- Click `Set as default` and disable `Open supported links` option
- Search for Aliucord & click on it
- Click `Set as default` and enable `Open supported links` option
- Click `Open supported links` and enable all of its options
